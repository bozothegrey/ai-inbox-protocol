# Mobile Capture Workflow

## Purpose

Capture text, links, screenshots, and OCR snippets from iPhone into the Notion `AI Inbox` system.

## Flow for text

Clipboard text  
↓  
Shortcut or Notion share sheet  
↓  
Create page in `AI Notes` database  
↓  
Set `Type = Mobile Capture` and `Status = Captured`

## Flow for screenshots

Screenshot / image  
↓  
Raw image stored in Google Drive if important  
↓  
OCR text extracted locally or by AI  
↓  
Notion capture note created in `AI Notes`

## Storage rule

Images and raw screenshots should not be stored in Notion by default.

Raw files live in Google Drive.

Notion stores processed notes, OCR, comments, links, and references to raw locations.
