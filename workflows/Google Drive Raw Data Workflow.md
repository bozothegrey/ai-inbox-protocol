# Google Drive Raw Data Workflow

## Purpose

Keep raw/source material outside Notion and Obsidian.

## Raw storage

Google Drive stores:

- PDFs
- screenshots
- article exports
- email exports
- transcripts
- source documents
- raw notes
- images

## Notion storage

Notion stores:

- processed summaries
- OCR text
- AI comments
- maps
- drafts
- indexes
- review notes
- promotion candidates

## Rule

Do not duplicate raw files into Notion or Obsidian by default.

When relevant, include a `Source / Raw Location` section pointing to the Google Drive location.
